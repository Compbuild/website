01 — The studio

Compbuild is a creative studio. We design and build realistic projects and worlds meant to last — choosing longevity over novelty, restraint over excess, and shared craft over individual ownership.

A brief history

2017 — Today
2017 Founded

A creative building project centered on exploration — learning through trial, error, and visual ideas.

2020 Realism & scale

Architectural detail and larger environments. The first large-scale builds pushed the project toward coherence.

2024 Intentional growth

Rapid, deliberate growth — clearer systems and a stronger emphasis on continuity and restraint.

2026 Compbuild today

An adaptable studio with its own workflows, UX, and a distinct creative identity — built for the long view.
